Welcome to my website

I am Boisali Biswas, a fiber artist based in Michigan. I focus on creating intricate and beautiful works of art using fiber as my medium. I bring to life my passion and creativity in every piece I create, and I hope you will enjoy them as much as I enjoy making them.

About the Artist

I was born and raised in India, where I completed my education at Visva Bharati University. My passion for fiber arts led me to pursue a Master's degree in the same at BGSU, Michigan. I have been honing my skills and perfecting my craft for many years, and I am now proud to share my work with the world.

I am deeply influenced by my heritage and the vibrant colors and patterns of India. I also find inspiration in nature and its intricate beauty. I hope to bring a unique and meaningful perspective to the fiber art community through my work.
